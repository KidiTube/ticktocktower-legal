## 7. Tower Mode and Hearts

Tower Mode uses a **hearts/lives system**:
- You start with a maximum of **5 hearts**
- Each new floor attempt costs **1 heart**
- Hearts regenerate at a rate of **1 heart every 20 minutes**
- You may spend **5 Gems** to instantly restore a heart (Gems are earned in-game, not purchased)
- Replaying a previously completed floor is **free** (no heart cost)

The heart regeneration system is subject to change at our discretion.
